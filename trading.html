Interbank Market Structure:
The interbank market structure is, in general terms, where huge banks transact with one another. The can transact directly through Electronic Brokering Services (EBS) or Thomson Reuters who are the two electronic brokerting platforms at this level. They basically provide an Order Book for all the huge banks, such as: Bank of America, UBS, HSBS, Deutsche Bank, JP Morgan Chase, Barclays, etc.
